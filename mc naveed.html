MC Naveed - Minecraft, formerly known as MC Naveed - Minecraft & Roblox and MORE!, or simply MC Naveed,is an English Minecraft YouTuber who does Minecraft videos daily. Starting his channel on March 15, 2013, he nonstop did videos, as well as Roblox videos on his other channel, RBNaveed. He usually does videos with his friendly zombie Mark. No one is sure when he was born or what his real name is, though it is known to most likely be Naveed.
